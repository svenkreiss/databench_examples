Second, the standard hello world example for frameworks like `angular.js` is tested below. The `simplepi` example that is also included here (in the other tabs) makes use of angular's data-binding. When a `status` signal is received from the Databench backend, the callback function does not use `jQuery` anymore to modify the DOM, but simply assigns the new estimate of \\(\\pi\\) to a variable of an angular controller, say `pi`.
